Abbreviations for Ġabra
Created by Zach, and edited by Julian

When using Ġabra to find conjugations for Maltese words, 
the website uses abbreviations in the 'Aspect' and 'Subject' 
columns that aren't explained. 

Zach has explained ALL abbreviations in the section below.
-----------------------------------------------------------

p1 First person (I, Me)
p2 Second person (You)
p3 Third person (He, She)
sg Singular (one person)
m Male form (he)
f Female form (she)
pl Plural (several people)
perf Perfect (past tense)
impf Imperfect (present tense)
pos Positive
